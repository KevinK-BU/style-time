How can I add clothes?
- Take a photo of the clothing item you want to add, with a high-contrast background. Double-check the clothing item is well-lit and clearly visible.
- Upload an image of the clothing item you want to add.
- Click the image to select the background color you want to remove. (This is why the high-contrast background is essential!)
- After the background is removed, you can enter the name and category of the clothing item.
- Click "Save To Closet" to add the clothing item to your closet.
- You can then click "Reset Canvas" to clear the canvas for the next item.
